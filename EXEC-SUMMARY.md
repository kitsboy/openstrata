# openstrata — Executive Summary

## Problem
Sovereign data portability and decentralized identity bridging remain fragmented across Bitcoin, Lightning, and Nostr protocols — users lack a unified standard to carry their identity, reputation, and data across sovereign digital domains.

## Solution
openstrata provides an open standard for sovereign data portability and decentralized identity bridging, enabling seamless interoperability across Bitcoin, Lightning, and Nostr protocols without sacrificing self-sovereignty.

## Traction
- Initial project scaffold and documentation deployed
- Open standard specification in development

## Market
Decentralized identity, sovereign data management, cross-protocol interoperability in the Bitcoin ecosystem.
