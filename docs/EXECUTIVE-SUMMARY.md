## One-Line Pitch

---

## The Problem

BC strata management is a **heavily regulated, labour-intensive** industry:

- BCFSA requires licensed brokerages, Managing Brokers, UBC Sauder certification
- Separate trust funds per corporation — co-mingling is illegal
- SPA mandates strict workflows (14-day bylaw windows, 7-day Form B, quorum rules)
- CRT resolves most disputes — evidence quality wins cases
- Credit cards cost strata **3%+** on fee collection ($9K+/year per 50-unit building)
- Managers spend **hours** on reconciliation, forms, and complaint tracking

Legacy software (Yardi, AppFolio equivalents for strata) is expensive, US-centric, and has **zero Bitcoin/sovereignty path**.

### Three Legal Go-To-Market Paths

1. **Licensed Brokerage Partner** — Hermes powers a BCFSA-licensed firm's operations
2. **Self-Managed Council** — Owners use Hermes directly (no license required for self-management)
3. **Hybrid** — Council runs day-to-day; licensed manager provides brokerage oversight

**We sell software. We do not provide unlicensed management services.**
