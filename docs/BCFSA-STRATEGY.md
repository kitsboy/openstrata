## What BCFSA Requires (The Moat)

To operate a strata **management company** in BC:

1. Register as **licensed brokerage** under Real Estate Services Act
2. Employ a **Managing Broker** (licensed, supervises all activities)
3. Every strata manager: **UBC Sauder course + BCFSA exam + license**
4. **Separate trust funds** per strata corporation
5. **Strict record-keeping** per RESA and SPA
6. **Written management agreement** before commencing services

**Exception:** Owners may **self-manage** their own strata without a license.

Sources:
- https://www.bcfsa.ca/industry-resources/real-estate-professional-resources/education-and-licensing/becoming-licensed/strata-management
- https://www.bcfsa.ca/industry-resources/real-estate-professional-resources/knowledge-base/strata-management-resources/working-with-strata-management-company

## How We Compete (Without Breaking Rules)

### We Are NOT a Management Company

Hermes Strata is **software infrastructure**. We do not:
- Hold ourselves out as a licensed brokerage
- Provide management services without a licensed entity
- Touch trust funds directly (we orchestrate; banks hold)
